BACKGROUND

Thembelihle "Fitsisto" Simelane is an aspiring eSwatini musician who came to this world as a second born of Absalom Simelane and Sibongile Simelane (nee Ndlangamandla) on the 29th of July 1985. He was born at Lucolweni (New Heaven/Velebantfu) but was raised at his native residence at kaMhawu (Ntshanini) residence, under the Ngudzeni constituency.

INTRODUCTION TO MUSIC

A lot of local artists had helped spark Fitsisto’s talent (career) since his introductory to music. He has worked by so many artists, including the likes of Fortune Ndlangamandla, Culprit, 3step, Nerves Tonics and Master Seth to name a few. Fitsisto (currently) mainly records under Chainlion Records (a home-based studio run by his younger brother Ngcebo Mandla Simelane) and has had an experience to perform at various house and road shows around the country. Under house shows, he has performed at some halls from places like Simunye, Msunduza and Zulwini (to name a few). Fitsisto has headlined events like the Ngudzeni Talent Show(Search) , where he serves as a coordinator as well alongside his brother (Chainlion) and people like Bheki Mkhumane who is a chair of the Ngudzeni Youth Council.

EXHIBITION

Since his showing up in the local music industry, Fitsisto has mainly released single hits and is currently working on an album (by popular demand).
